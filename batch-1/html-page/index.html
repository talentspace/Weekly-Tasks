What we learned?

1 Nov 2015

Apart from learning the fact that we have found our 'Sensei' who will show us the way of code.

We also did happen to learn a quite number of things for an introductory class.

Summing it up would be:

  • curl - facilitates the transfer of a URL.
  • man - Documentations at your service.
  • scp - helps to do your file copy across systems.
  • grep - fetches and prints the lines matching a pattern, neat huh?
  • wc - counts up and prints the newline, word and byte for each of the files!
  • ssh - get access to servers anywhere on the planet, provided you are authorised.
  • wget - download things off the web with no questions asked.
  • youtube-dl - download off YouTube videos or the whole playlists using this badboy.
  • history - pondering over what all you've typed in the terminal? This is the answer.
